Chapter 6.4, Problem 19E
Problem

a. Assuming that the following sentence is a statement, prove that 1 + 1 = 3:

If this sentence is true, then 1 + 1 = 3.

b. What can you deduce from part (a) about the status of “This sentence is true”? Why? (This example is known as Löb’s paradox.)

    (a)

    Consider that the following sentence is a statement.

    “If this sentence is true, then ”
