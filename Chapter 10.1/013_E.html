Chapter 10.1, Problem 13E
Problem

Solve the vegetarians-and-cannibals puzzle for the case where there are three vegetarians and three cannibals to be transported from one side of a river to the other.

Step-by-step solution
  1. Step 1 of 6

    Let us consider the following puzzle.

    Consider an island on which all the people are of one of two types, either vegetarians or cannibals.

    Initially, three vegetarians and three cannibals are on the left bank of a river.

    Now, they have a boat that can hold a maximum of two people.

    The aim is to find a way to transport all the vegetarians and cannibals to the right bank of the river.

    But the condition is at no time the number of cannibals on either bank should be more than the number of vegetarians.
