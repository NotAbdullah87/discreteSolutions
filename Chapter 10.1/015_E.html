Problem

A graph has vertices of degrees 0, 2, 2, 3, and 9. How many edges does the graph have?

Step-by-step solution
  1. Step 1 of 1

    Let G be a graph, which has vertices of degrees 0, 2, 2, 3, and 9.

    Then, the total degree of the graph G is 0 + 2 + 2 + 3 + 9 = 16.

    By a known theorem, the total degree of a graph is twice the number of edges.

    The number of edges of the graph G is 8.
