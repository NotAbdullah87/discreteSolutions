Chapter 3.4, Problem 24E
Problem

Indicate whether the arguments are valid or invalid. Support your answers by drawing diagrams.

Exercise

No vegetarians eat meat.

All vegans are vegetarian.

∴ No vegans eat m.eat.

Step-by-step solution
  1. Step 1 of 1

    The argument is rewritten as

    All vegans are vegetarians If he is a vegan, then he is a vegetarian

    No vegetarians eat meat If he is a vegetarian, then he does not eat meat

    No vegans eat meat If he is a vegan, then he will not eat meat

    This argument can be written symbolically as

    V(x): vegan, G(x): vegetarian, M(x): eats meat

    This is a valid argument by universal transitivity
