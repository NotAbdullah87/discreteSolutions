Chapter 8.3, Problem 31E
Problem

(1) prove that the relation is an equivalence relation, and (2) describe the distinct equivalence classes of each relation.

Exercise

Let P be the set of all points in the Cartesian plane except the origin. R is the relation defined on P as follows: For all p1 and p2 in P,

p1 R p2 ⇔ p1 and p2 lie on the same half-line emanating from the origin.
