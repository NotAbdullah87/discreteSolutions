Problem

One urn contains 10 red balls and 25 green balls, and a second urn contains 22 red balls and 15 green balls. A ball is chosen as follows: First an urn is selected by tossing a loaded coin with probability 0.4 of landing heads up and probability 0.6 of landing tails up. If the coin lands heads up, the first urn is chosen; otherwise, the second urn is chosen. Then a ball is picked at random from the chosen urn.

a. What is the probability that the chosen ball is green?

b. If the chosen ball is green, what is the probability that it was picked from the first urn?
