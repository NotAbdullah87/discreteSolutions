  2. Step 2 of 3

    To draw the diagram, place each subarray in two columns, one at the left and one at the right. Along the bottom of the diagram, set 10 positions into which the elements of the subarray will be moved. Now, one-by-one, bring down the elements from the bottoms of the two columns. At each stage compare the elements currently at the column bottoms and move the element which is smaller down into the next position in the array as a whole.

    Step 1

    Compare the alphabet F and C at the bottom of the two columns, since C is smaller than F, move C in the final array at the first position.

    Step 2

    Similarly, all the elements of the two columns are set into the final position as per the procedure described in the step 1 and step 2. It is to be noted that, when the two elements are same, use the convention, say, the element in the left array moves to the next position in the final array.
