    To draw the diagram, place each subarray in two columns, one at the left and one at the right. Along the bottom of the diagram, setup 10 positions into which the elements of the subarray will be moved. Now, one-by-one, bring down the elements from the bottoms of the two columns. At each stage compare the elements currently at the column bottoms and move the element which is smaller down into the next position in the array as a whole. The following explanation explains the procedure of merge algorithm applied on the given array:

    Step 1

    Compare the number 3 and 2 at the bottom of the two columns, since 2 is smaller than 3, move 2 in the final array at the first position.

    Similarly, all the elements of the two columns are set into the final positions using the procedure described in the step 1 and step 2.
