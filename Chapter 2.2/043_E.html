Chapter 2.2, Problem 43E
Problem

Use the contrapositive to rewrite the statements in if-then form in two ways.

Exercise

Doing homework regularly is a necessary condition for Jim to pass the course.

Step-by-step solution
  1. Step 1 of 1

    The objective is to write the following statement using contrapositive in if-then form in two ways:

    “Doing homework regularly is a necessary condition for Jim to pass the course.”

    Rewrite the statement in if-then form.

    “If Jim passes the course, then he is doing homework regularly.”

    The contrapositive statement for is

    The statement using contrapositive definition is as follows:

    “If Jim is not doing homework regularly, then Jim will not pass the course.”

    Another form of contrapositive statement is,

    “If Jim passes the course, then Jim is doing homework regularly.”
