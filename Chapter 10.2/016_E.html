Chapter 10.2, Problem 16E
Problem

Determine which of the graphs in have Euler circuits. If the graph does not have an Euler circuit, explain why not. If it does have an Euler circuit, describe one.

Step-by-step solution
  1. Step 1 of 1

    We know that a graph G has a Euler circuit if and only if G is connected and every vertex of G has an even degree.

    The given graph is not connected because there is no walk from the vertex.

    Thus, the graph has no Euler circuit.
