Problem

An edge whose removal disconnects the graph of which it is a part is called a bridge. Find all bridges for each of the following graphs.

a.

b.

c.

Step-by-step solution
  1. Step 1 of 3

    (a) are bridges. If one of these edges is removed, the graph is disconnected.

  2. Step 2 of 3

    (b) are bridges. If any one of these edges is removed from the graph, the graph is disconnected.

  3. Step 3 of 3

    (c) are bridges, and every one of the above edges disconnects the graph if it is removed.
