Step-by-step solution
  1. Step 1 of 1

    Find a Hamiltonian circuit that minimizes the total distance traveled.

    Some possible Hamiltonian circuits are as follows:

    1. Brussels- Luxembourg- Dusseldorf- Paris-- Berlin- Brussels.

    Total distance traveled by the visitor is km.

    2. Brussels-Luxembourg- Paris-Munich-Berlin-Dusseldorf-Brussels.

    Total distance traveled by the visitor is km.

    3. Brussels-Paris-Luxembourg-Munich-Berlin-Dusseldorf-Brussels.

    Total distance traveled by the visitor is km.

    Therefore, the Hamiltonian circuit is Brussels-Paris-Luxembourg-Munich-Berlin-Dusseldorf-Brussels in which total distance is minimum.
