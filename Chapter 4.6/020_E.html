Chapter 4.6, Problem 20E
Problem

Prove the statement by contraposition.

Exercise

If a sum of two real numbers is less than 50, then at least one of the numbers is less than 25.

Step-by-step solution
  1. Step 1 of 2

    The objective is to prove the following by contraposition:

    If a sum of two real numbers is less than 50, then at least one of the n umbers is less than 25.

    Contrapositive statement:

    If there are two real numbers, neither of which is less than 25, then their sum will be greater than or equal to 50.
