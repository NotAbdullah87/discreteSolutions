Chapter 10.6, Problem 8E
Problem

In each of either draw a graph with the given specifications or explain why no such graph exists.

Exercise

Full binary tree, nine vertices

Step-by-step solution
  1. Step 1 of 2

    Each parent vertex in a full binary tree has exactly two children and it never contain more than two children.

    An internal vertex of a full binary tree has exactly two siblings.

    The nine vertices can be arranged with four internal vertices and four terminal vertices.
