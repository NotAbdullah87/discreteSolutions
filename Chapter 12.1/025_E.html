Chapter 12.1, Problem 25E
Problem

In 25–27 find a regular expression that defines the given language.

The language consisting of all strings of 0’s and 1’s with an odd number of 1’s. (Such a string is said to have odd parity.)

Step-by-step solution
  1. Step 1 of 1

    It is required to find a regular expression that defines the given language in words “The language consisting of 0’s and 1’s with an odd number of 1’s”. One of the possible solution for the above statement is

    The part consist of strings of 0’s and exactly one 1. The second part consist of which is set of all strings consisting of 0’s and exactly two 1’s and this expression is closed under concatenation, so the strings obtained will always be even and if 1 is added to even number of 1’s then number of 1’s become odd in number.
