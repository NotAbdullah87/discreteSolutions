Chapter 4.4, Problem 11E
Problem

Check the correctness of formula for the following values of DayT and N.

Formula

DayN = (DayT + N) mod 7,

a. DayT = 6 (Saturday) and N = 15

b. DayT = 0 (Sunday) and N = 7

c. DayT = 4 (Thursday) and N = 12

Step-by-step solution
  1. Step 1 of 3

    If day T is the day of the week today, and day N is the day of the week in N days,

    Then, day N = (day T + N) mod 7

    Where Sunday = 0, Monday = 1, Tuesday = 2, Wednesday = 3, Thursday = 4, Friday = 5, Saturday = 6.

  2. Step 2 of 3

    (a) Day T = 6 (Saturday) and N = 15

    Because

    Thus, the day of the week in 15 days is Sunday

  3. Step 3 of 3

    (b) Day T = 0 (Saturday) and N = 7

    Because

    Thus, if today is Sunday, then the day of the week in 7 days is Sunday

    (c) Day T = 4 (Thursday) and N = 12

    Because

    Thus, if today is Thursday, then the day of the week in 12 days is Tuesday
